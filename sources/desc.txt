Here is the list of the 9 core domains and their symbol descriptions in plain text:

Arcana: A wide, upright eye with radiating lines, representing magical vision.

Blade: A sword pointing downward with a pair of wings extending from the hilt.

Bone: A stylized, geometric skull representing physical grit and mortality.

Codex: An open book with a flame or magical spark rising from the center.

Grace: An elegant, stylized swan or bird in profile, representing poise.

Midnight: A crescent moon partially encircling a dark star or orb.

Sage: A single, detailed leaf with organic veins, representing nature.

Splendor: A radiant sun or a sparkling diamond emitting light.

Valor: A heavy, vertical shield used to symbolize protection and defense.

Additionally, the newer Dread domain is represented by a cracked or shattered mask.
